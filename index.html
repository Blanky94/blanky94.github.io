Improved Learning offers a different tutoring experience.

Offering group sessions or one on one lessons, our tutors take the time to analyse how a student best learns and prioritise what the student needs through discussion and basic worksheets that determine how to best help reach their goals. This is based off of the fact that no two students learn the same way. Often, students may become frustrated and deflated when they find themselves unable to keep up in the class. It is important to remember that this does not necessarily indicate that the student is lacking in academic skill or understanding, rather that they have yet to have the content explained to them in a way that they can comprehend.
