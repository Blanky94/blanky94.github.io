About Our Tutors

Our team has a combined experience of eleven years of tutoring, with backgrounds in Education, Science, and Engineering.

Experience

Between them, the tutors employed have attended Curtin University, Edith Cowan University and the University of Western Australia. Our team has a combined experience of eleven years of tutoring. They have worked in Educational institutions as teaching assistants, practicum teachers and in administrative roles that has allowed them to develop their skills as tutors and become familiar with classroom settings and the academia found within. *Note: All tutors employed have obtained a Working With Children Check.

Qualifications

The tutors employed are University graduates in the areas of Bachelor of Education (Secondary), Bachelor of Arts (Writing), and Bachelor of Science (Engineering). All tutors have undergone WACE exams as an entry pathway into University. Each has worked as either a practicum teacher or teaching assistant within classrooms.
